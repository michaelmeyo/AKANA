Why AKANA

Empowering Kenyan Nurse Anesthetists

We unite and uplift Kenyan-origin nurse anesthetists across the U.S. and Kenya through mentorship, advocacy, continuing education, and global collaboration.

Global & Connected

Bridging Kenya and the Diaspora

AKANA connects professionals in North America with colleagues and training institutions in Kenya, advancing anesthesia practice, research, and leadership capacity worldwide.
