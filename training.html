How the Certificate Courses Work

Each course consists of a brief learning module (slides, video, or reading) followed by a 10-question assessment. Upon scoring 80% or higher, you will instantly receive a personalized certificate of completion (PDF) via email.

AKANA members' course completions are tracked in your profile, so you can download your certificates anytime. These certificates may be used for CPD/CME credits as applicable.
